Look at the <head> and <body>

The <head> contains metadata (title, meta, links, scripts). The <body> contains visible content like this text and images.

Quick code summary

<head> — <title>, <meta>, <link>, <script> (metadata)
<body> — visible content (<h1>, <p>, <img>, <form>)
